# Side Income Execution Plan

Working date: 2026-06-07.

Goal: start serious side-income execution from Monday, 2026-06-08, with a small service offer and a public-safe technical demo.

## Positioning

Recommended positioning:

> Small workflow automation for teams using kintone, Slack, Google Sheets, CSV, and manual admin processes.

Why this is the right MVP:

- It matches existing technical strengths.
- It can be sold as a small service before building a SaaS.
- It avoids heavy infra, auth, billing, and customer-data risk.
- It gives partner agencies something concrete to review.

## Channel Priority

| Priority | Channel | Why | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Direct outreach to kintone partners/agencies | Best fit for small subcontract work and faster trust path. | Needs clear portfolio and conservative wording. |
| 2 | Freelance platforms / side-job platforms | Existing demand signal for kintone support. | Platform fees, competition, and closed listings. |
| 3 | Direct SMB outreach | Higher margin if trust is built. | Harder cold-start without referrals. |
| 4 | Digital templates on BOOTH/Gumroad/note | Good later as productized assets. | Slower income path; needs audience/distribution. |

## First Week MVP

### Day 1: Foundation

- Finish this repo.
- Prepare GitHub profile README draft.
- Prepare one Notion or simple landing page using `landing/index.md`.
- Create a short portfolio link list.
- Make a list of 20 target partners/agencies.

### Day 2: Outreach Batch 1

- Send 5 partner/agency emails.
- Register or update profile on one side-job/freelance platform.
- Apply to 1-2 relevant kintone support listings if available.

### Day 3: Demo Polish

- Record a short demo GIF or screenshots using fake data.
- Add a short case-study style explanation.
- Tighten service package wording.

### Day 4-5: Outreach Batch 2

- Send 5 more partner/agency emails.
- Send 3 SMB outreach messages if the target has visible kintone usage or operational DX need.
- Follow up on any replies within 24 hours.

### Day 6-7: Review

- Count sent messages, replies, calls booked, and objections.
- Improve templates based on actual replies.
- Decide whether to publish a small paid template later.

## Daily Minimum

For the first two weeks:

- 3 targeted outreach messages per day.
- 1 small asset improvement per day.
- 1 short note about what worked or did not work.

## Done Criteria For The First Two Weeks

- 30 targeted outreach messages sent.
- 3 platform/profile applications completed.
- 1 clear landing page or Notion page published.
- 1 demo repo public or ready to publish.
- At least 1 call or meaningful reply booked.

If no replies after 30 targeted messages, adjust positioning before adding more demos.
